# Formula Booklet - Asset Liability Management

## FI Spread & ALM
Net interest margin
$$NIM = \frac{\text{Interest on assets} - \text{cost of liabilities}}{\text{Assets}} = \frac{\text{NII}}{\text{assets}}$$

Net underwriting margin
$$\text{Net Underwriting Margin} = \frac{\text{Premium Income} - \text{Policy Expenses}}{\text{Assets}}$$
